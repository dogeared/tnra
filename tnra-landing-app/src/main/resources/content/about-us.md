# About TNRA

*Taking the Next Right Action* started over 23 years ago by a group of men - all of whom had long-term, twelve-step
recovery. The idea was simple: we could hold each other accountable to improve our personal, 
family and professional lives. This would only enhance our recovery.

## Who we are

TNRA is built for groups that **mean it** — recovery circles, faith communities,
and professional accountability teams who show up for one another week after week.

> "Accountability isn't about catching people doing wrong. It's about helping
> each other do the next right thing."

## What we believe

- **Consistency over intensity.** Small, repeated actions compound.
- **Privacy by default.** Your group's words belong to your group.
- **Structure is freedom.** A shared format removes friction and excuses.

## How we got here

The first version was a weekly email thread. It worked, barely. We grew by making use of email groups, like Yahoo
and Google Groups. We then started using Slack and eventually a custom web application (that still integrates with 
Slack).

The web app has grown into a modern, secure and easy to use application that helps our original TNRA group with the 
weekly cadence of posting what's happening in our lives. We believe that other groups will find the TNRA Way useful
and so we're releasing the application for general availability for your accountability group.

TNRA is what happens when you take that ritual seriously and give it a real home.

For more on the rhythm we recommend, see [**The TNRA Way**](/) on our home page —
covering the `Daily`, `Weekly`, `Monthly`, and `Yearly` cadence.
