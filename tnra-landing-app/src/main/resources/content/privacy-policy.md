# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: June 21, 2026*

This Privacy Policy explains what information *Taking the Next Right Action* ("TNRA", "we", "us")
collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to the TNRA web application and the
public website at tnra.app.

## Information we collect

- **Account information.** Your name, email address, and (optionally) a phone number and profile
  photo. Accounts are created when a group administrator invites you, or when you sign in through
  your group's identity provider.
- **Content you create.** The posts, stats, and notes you write in the app. This content belongs to
  you and your group.
- **Usage information.** Basic technical data such as log entries and timestamps needed to operate and
  secure the service.

## Payment information

Payments are processed by **Lemon Squeezy**, our Merchant of Record. When you subscribe or gift a
membership, your card details are entered on Lemon Squeezy's hosted checkout and are handled by them —
**TNRA never receives or stores your full card number.** We retain only a billing record (such as your
membership status and the email associated with a subscription) needed to grant access. See Lemon
Squeezy's privacy policy for how they handle payment data.

## How we use your information

We use your information to:

- provide the service and your group's accountability features;
- authenticate you and keep your account secure;
- send service email (for example, access requests, notifications you've opted into, and important
  account or billing notices);
- operate, maintain, and improve the product.

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do not use your post content for advertising.

## How your data is protected

- **Encryption at rest.** Sensitive post content is encrypted in our database using AES-256-GCM before
  it is written to disk.
- **Encryption in transit.** Traffic to the site and app is served over HTTPS.
- **Authentication.** Sign-in is handled by a dedicated identity provider; we never see your password.

## Service providers

We share information only with the providers needed to run TNRA, and only as required to deliver the
service:

- **Lemon Squeezy** — payments and subscription billing (Merchant of Record).
- **Mailgun** — transactional email delivery.
- **Our hosting provider** — to run the application and database.

We may also disclose information if required by law.

## Your choices

- **Access and update.** You can view and edit your profile in the app.
- **Export.** You can export your own post data from your profile.
- **Deletion.** To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us (below). Some
  records may be retained where required for legal, accounting, or security reasons.

## Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service,
then for any period required to meet legal and accounting obligations.

## Children

TNRA is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
information from children.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated"
date above and, where appropriate, communicated to you.

## Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email **support@dogeared.dev**.
