# Refund Policy

*Last updated: June 21, 2026*

We want TNRA to be worth it. This policy explains how cancellations and refunds work for memberships.

## Subscriptions

TNRA memberships are billed monthly ($7) or yearly ($60) per member and renew automatically until
cancelled. Purchases are processed by **Lemon Squeezy**, our Merchant of Record.

## Cancelling

You can cancel anytime from the membership screen — open **"Update payment method"** to reach the
billing portal and cancel. When you cancel:

- you are not charged again;
- your access continues through the end of the period you've already paid for.

## Refunds

- **14-day refund window.** If you're not satisfied, contact us within **14 days** of a charge and
  we'll work with you on a refund of that charge.
- **Renewals.** Because cancelling stops future billing and you keep access through the paid period, we
  generally don't refund partial or unused time on a renewal — but reach out if something went wrong
  (for example, you were charged after intending to cancel) and we'll make it right.
- **How refunds are issued.** Refunds are processed by Lemon Squeezy back to your original payment
  method. Your purchase is also subject to Lemon Squeezy's refund terms.

## Gifted memberships

If you gifted a membership to another member, you are the payer and may request a refund under the same
terms above. Refunding or cancelling a gift stops future billing to you; the recipient's access
continues through any period already paid.

## How to request a refund

Email **support@dogeared.dev** from the address associated with your account, and include the approximate
date of the charge. We typically respond within a couple of business days.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated"
date above.
