Here are the commands you can use to post:

    /start
        start a new post

    /finish
        finish a post

    /show [<username>]
        show your in-progress post OR your or someone else's last finished post

    /wid <text>
        update WIDWYTK

    /kry <text>
        update Kryptonite

    /wha
        update What and When

    /per [bes|wor] <text>
        update personal best or worst

    /fam [bes|wor] <text>
        update family best or worst

    /wor [bes|wor] <text>
        update work best or worst

    /sta [exe:<num>] [gtg:<num>] [med:<num>] [mee:<num>] [pra:<num>] [rea:<num>] [spo:<num>]
        update stats. Each available stat can be listed in any combination and in any order

    /exe <num>
        update exercise stat

    /gtg <num>
        update go-to-guy stat

    /med <num>
        update meditation stat

    /mee <num>
        update meeting stat

    /pra <num>
        update pray stat

    /rea <num>
        update read stat

    /spo <num>
        update sponsor stat


    Commands long forms:

    /post [help|start|finish]
    /post show <@user>
    /post [update|replace|append] intro [widwytk|kryptonite|whatandwhen] <text>
    /post [update|replace|append] [personal|family|work] [best|work] <text>
    /post update stats [exercise:<num>] [gtg:<num>] [meditate:<num>] [meetings:<num>] [pray:<num>] [read:<num>] [sponsor:<num>]

Notes:

    * you can only have one in-progress post at a time
    * when you finish a post, all sections and stats must be complete or you will get an error
    * the date and time is recorded when you start a post
    * the date and time is recorded when you finish a post
